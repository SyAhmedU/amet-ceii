A harbour built for
the blue economy.

AMET CEII is the entrepreneurship, innovation and incubation centre of AMET University — India's pioneering maritime institution. We help founders turn sea-going ideas into seaworthy companies.

Our vision

Foster entrepreneurship & innovation with a focus on the maritime sector

We back business ideas that strengthen the blue economy — boosting economic contribution from shipping, ports, ocean-tech, aquaculture and coastal industries.

Our mission

Empower young innovators to navigate the startup journey

By fostering creative thinking and providing essential support, guidance and resources, we help founders navigate challenges and achieve sustainable growth throughout their startup journey.

The story

Deep expertise,
modern tempo.

AMET University has spent decades training the officers, engineers and leaders who run the world's ships. CEII channels that hard-won maritime credibility into something new: a founder-first incubator where domain knowledge meets venture-building.

The result is a place where a coastal-sustainability idea can meet a marine engineer, a port operator and an angel investor in the same week. We don't just give founders a desk — we give them charts, a crew, and a fair wind.

CEII operates alongside the university's Institution's Innovation Council (IIC), feeding student energy into a real venture pipeline and connecting the campus to India's wider innovation ecosystem.

What we hold to

Principles that steer us

Founder-first

The founder is the captain. Our job is to crew, provision and chart — never to take the wheel.

Real over hype

Pilots with real ports and customers beat pitch-deck theatre. We optimise for seaworthiness.

Blue & sustainable

The ocean is a shared commons. We favour ventures that grow the economy without depleting the sea.

Open harbour

Talent finds us from every discipline and background. The gangway is always down.

Evidence-led

Decisions ride on data and domain truth, not fashion. We measure what we claim.

Community current

Alumni mentor the next cohort. The network is the moat, and it compounds.

Part of AMET University

CEII is a unit of AMET University, Chennai — an institution devoted to maritime education, training and research.
