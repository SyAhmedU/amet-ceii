AMET CEII is the entrepreneurship, innovation and incubation centre of AMET University. We help students and faculty from across all 18 departments turn ideas into real companies.

Our vision

“To play a commendable role in fostering Entrepreneurship and Innovation, thereby providing a platform for incubating business ideas in the maritime sector and augmenting its contribution to the economy.”

Our mission

“To empower young innovators by fostering creative thinking and providing essential support, guidance, and resources to help them navigate challenges and achieve sustainable growth during their startup journey.”

The story

Deep expertise,
modern tempo.

AMET University spans 18 departments and thousands of students across engineering, management, computer science and the sciences. CEII channels that breadth into something new: a founder-first incubator open to every discipline, where domain knowledge meets venture-building.

The result is a place where a healthtech idea can meet an engineer, a designer and an angel investor in the same week. We don't just give founders a desk — we give them mentors, a network and a real path to build.

CEII operates alongside the university's Institution's Innovation Council (IIC), feeding student energy into a real venture pipeline and connecting the campus to India's wider innovation ecosystem.

What we hold to

Principles that steer us

Founder-first

The founder leads. Our job is to support, resource and guide — never to take over.

Real over hype

Pilots with real ports and customers beat pitch-deck theatre. We optimise for seaworthiness.

Responsible & sustainable

We favour ventures that create real value without cutting corners on people or the planet.

Open door

Talent finds us from every department and background. The door is always open.

Evidence-led

Decisions ride on data and domain truth, not fashion. We measure what we claim.

Community current

Alumni mentor the next cohort. The network is the moat, and it compounds.

Part of AMET University

CEII is a unit of AMET University, Chennai — an institution devoted to maritime education, training and research.
