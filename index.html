Why a maritime harbour

The blue economy is the
next great frontier — and
it needs its own harbour.

Ninety percent of world trade moves by sea, yet maritime innovation is chronically under-incubated. CEII exists to close that gap: a founder-first environment purpose-built for shipping, ports, ocean-tech, aquaculture, coastal sustainability and the logistics that bind them.

We combine the deep domain credibility of AMET University — India's pioneering maritime institution — with the tempo of a modern venture studio. Founders get more than desks: charts, mentors, capital introductions, and a fair wind.
